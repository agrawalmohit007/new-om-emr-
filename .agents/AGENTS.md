# Customization Rules: Clinical Guidelines Priority

This project follows a strict clinical guidelines hierarchy for EMR AI diagnostics, insights, and look-ahead alerts:
$$\text{FOGSI (India)} > \text{ACOG (US)} > \text{RCOG (UK)}$$

---

## 1. Gestational Diabetes Mellitus (GDM)
*   **Protocol**: **DIPSI (FOGSI) Single-Step Method** (Primary).
*   **Criteria**:
    *   Perform a 75g non-fasting GCT at 24-28 weeks for all prenatal checks.
    *   2-hour plasma glucose threshold $\ge 140 \text{ mg/dL}$ diagnoses GDM.
    *   MNT (Medical Nutrition Therapy) for 2 weeks first line. Metformin or Insulin is suggested if post-meal sugar $\ge 120\text{ mg/dL}$ after MNT.

## 2. Preeclampsia Prophylaxis (Aspirin)
*   **Protocol**: **FOGSI GCPR** (150 mg Aspirin daily starting at 12-16 weeks until birth).
*   **Aspirin Risk Indicators**:
    *   **High Risk (1 required)**: Chronic HTN, pre-eclampsia history, CKD, autoimmune disease, pre-gestational diabetes.
    *   **Moderate Risk (2 required)**: First pregnancy, age $\ge 40$, interval $> 10$ years, BMI $\ge 35$, family history of pre-eclampsia, twins.

## 3. Anemia in Pregnancy
*   **Protocol**: **FOGSI Anemia Management**.
*   **Thresholds**:
    *   Hb $< 11 \text{ g/dL}$ (1st/3rd trimester), $< 10.5 \text{ g/dL}$ (2nd trimester).
    *   Mild/Moderate: Suggest 100 mg elemental oral iron twice daily.
    *   Severe/Postpartum: Suggest IV Iron Sucrose or FCM infusion.

## 4. PCOS Diagnosis
*   **Protocol**: **Rotterdam Consensus**.
*   **Criteria**: Diagnose if any 2 of 3 are present:
    *   Oligomenorrhea / Amenorrhea.
    *   Clinical or biochemical hyperandrogenism.
    *   Ultrasound showing $\ge 12$ follicles of 2-9mm or ovarian volume $> 10\text{ mL}$.

## 5. Cervical Cancer Screening
*   **Protocol**: **ACOG Guidelines**.
*   **Timeline**:
    *   21-29 years: Pap smear cytology alone every 3 years.
    *   30-65 years: Primary hrHPV screening every 5 years (preferred) or co-testing every 5 years.
